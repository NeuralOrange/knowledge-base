# RAG

<!-- kb:status Draft -->

> A technique that combines retrieval of relevant documents with generative AI
> to produce grounded, factual responses.

## 一句话理解

RAG gives AI a bookshelf — instead of relying on training data alone,
the model looks up relevant documents before answering.
